# Welcome to SlasshyWispr

SlasshyWispr is a desktop voice dictation and AI assistant app built for Windows.

Speak naturally, get clean transcription, generate AI responses, and hear replies with built-in text-to-speech.

<Note>
SlasshyWispr is in **strong beta**. Expect bugs, rough edges, and breaking changes while features are actively evolving.
</Note>

## What is SlasshyWispr?

SlasshyWispr transforms your voice into text with advanced AI assistance. Whether you need fast dictation for writing, hands-free interaction with your computer, or an intelligent assistant to help with tasks, SlasshyWispr provides a seamless voice-first experience.

### Key Capabilities

**Voice Dictation**
- Fast, accurate speech-to-text with customizable hotkeys
- Push-to-talk or single-tap capture modes
- Clipboard-friendly workflow with automatic paste options

**AI Assistant Mode**
- Wake phrase activation (e.g., "Hey Jarvis...")
- Context-aware responses with selection rewriting
- Built-in TTS playback for assistant responses

**Flexible Model Routing**
- **Online**: Use cloud-based APIs for maximum accuracy
- **Offline**: Run everything locally for privacy and offline access
- **Hybrid**: Mix and match online and offline models

## Why SlasshyWispr?

**Privacy First**: With offline model support, your voice data never leaves your machine.

**Flexible & Powerful**: Choose between speed (online models) and privacy (offline models), or use both.

**Developer Friendly**: Built with Tauri and TypeScript, with an active development community.

**Continuously Evolving**: Regular updates bring new features, models, and improvements.
